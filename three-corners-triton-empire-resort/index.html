Three corners Triton Empire Resort,Hurghada:

The Three Corners Triton Empire Beach Resort located in downtown in Al-Dahar district around the corner, The Triton Empire Beach Resort features a private sandy & pebbled beach. It also has 3 outdoor pools, a restaurant and large sun deck.

Rooms at the Three Corners Beach Resort include balconies with views of the garden or Red Sea. Each air-conditioned room comes equipped with a satellite TV and small refrigerator.
The Chez Pascal Restaurant offers Belgian cuisine with a choice of steaks, fresh seafood and lobster dishes. Peanuts Bar offers international and regional cuisine in a lively setting.
Guests can enjoy a massage on the beach, or participate in an organized diving trip. The resort has activities for children and offers excursions in a glass-bottom boat.
